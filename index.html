Hello,

Welcome to Anna Meyrick Mosaics, where every piece reflects a connection to nature, history, and spiritual symbolism. From stained glass spirit animals to intricate landscapes to personalized altar pieces, my work brings depth and character to each creation, telling stories that resonate.

This season, I’m now accepting custom orders for one-of-a-kind gifts. Whether inspired by nature, animals, or something personal to you, I can create something uniquely meaningful. In addition to custom work, I have exciting news! I’m in the process of building a new mosaic and ceramic studio, set to open in summer 2025. This dedicated space will allow for regular classes and workshops, and I look forward to welcoming you there soon.

Until then, I’m planning to host mosaic workshops in Eugene, Oregon, sometime this winter when there is enough interest in a specific date. If you are interested in taking a mosaic workshop in Eugene, please reach out to me and let me know dates that work for you. Mosaic workshops span two days (10 hours total), where participants will create a 12”x12” mosaic in the subject of their choice. All supplies are provided, and I’ll be guiding each step to bring your artistic vision to life.

If you’re interested in bringing a mosaic class to your area, I’ll happily travel to groups of 5 or more, ensuring a convenient and creative experience in your own community. Please reach out for custom orders, upcoming classes, or if you’d like to gather friends for a class in your area. I look forward to connecting and creating with you soon!

Warm regards,

Anna Meyrick,
